Configure Your Mining Hardware

×

Select your miner type to view configuration details:

IceRiver Miners

Connection Details:

Stratum URL: stratum+tcp://pool.kaspafunding.com:7777
Wallet: kaspa:your_wallet_address
Password: x (or leave blank)

To configure:

  1. Access your miner's web interface (usually http://miner-ip)
  2. Navigate to the Mining Configuration section
  3. Enter the pool details above
  4. Save settings and restart the miner

Goldshell Miners

Web Interface Settings:

Pool URL: pool.kaspafunding.com
Port: 7777
Worker: kaspa:your_wallet_address.worker_name
Password: 123

To configure:

  1. Access your miner's web interface
  2. Go to the Miner Configuration page
  3. Add a new pool with the details above
  4. Set as primary pool and apply changes

Custom Miners (HiveOS, GMiner, etc)

Example Configuration:

./kaspaminer --server pool.kaspafunding.com --port 7777 \ --user kaspa:your_wallet_address --pass x

For HiveOS:

  1. Create a new flight sheet
  2. Select your Kaspa miner
  3. Enter pool details: pool.kaspafunding.com:7777
  4. Wallet format: kaspa:your_address
  5. Save and apply to your workers

Verify Your Connection

×

After configuring your miner, follow these steps to verify your connection:

1. Check Miner Status

  • Wait 2-5 minutes for initial connection
  • Check your miner's dashboard for accepted shares
  • Verify the pool URL is correct in your miner's status

2. Check Pool Dashboard

  • Enter your wallet address in the search box above
  • Check if your workers appear in "Your Workers" section
  • Monitor your hashrate and shares over time

3. Troubleshooting

If you don't see your worker:

  • Double-check your wallet address format
  • Verify your miner is pointing to the correct pool URL
  • Check your internet connection and firewall settings
  • Restart your miner if needed

Connection Notes

It may take up to 10 minutes for new workers to appear in the dashboard
Worker names are case-sensitive (e.g., Worker1 vs worker1)
